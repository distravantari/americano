Tennis Sport Fun

Americano is all about everyone playing everyone! Rack up points by winning individual points in matches set to a certain games, like 4/6 games per match. Every games you win scores one for you and your partner.

Let's check out an example!

Imagine we have four players ready to compete in this Americano tennis game you've set up.

Meet our players: Fuji, Dimas, Apit, and Febri. They'll compete in three exciting rounds of Americano tennis.

Rounds Schedule

  1. First Round: Fuji & Dimas vs. Apit & Febri
  2. Second Round: Fuji & Apit vs. Dimas & Febri
  3. Last Round: Fuji & Febri vs. Dimas & Apit

How do we calculate scores based on the games each player wins?

In the first round, Fuji & Dimas won 4-2 against Apit & Febri. Hereâ€™s how the scores are distributed:

  • Fuji: 4 points
  • Dimas: 4 points
  • Apit: 2 points
  • Febri: 2 points

With the first round complete, we move on to the next rounds.

Conclusion

With Americano tennis, every player gets equal game time, faces every opponent, and teams up with each player. It's an exhilarating way to play tennis! OHT has been hosting these games for nearly a year, and we're still loving every match.

We hope you'll enjoy this dynamic style of tennis as much as we do!
